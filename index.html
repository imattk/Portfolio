About Me

I am passionate web developer who is motivated by design and project completion. I Started the coding journey with zero knowledge and quickly learned the skills needed to design and develop websites. I am Eager to learn and develop knowledge of software tools and empower concepts for creating that perfect project. I enjoy working in a group setting as well as individually on projects. Appreciates building UX and UI's that solve simple and complex problems and are delightful to use on both desktop and mobile devices.

Currently working toward my Associate Degree in Computer Science and maintaining a 4.0 GPA. Through classes such as IT140 and IT145, that taught me programming languages such as Python and Java, I began to appreciate back end programming and the intricacies of how the behind the scenes programming works.

Developing a passion for programming and fully immersing myself into the programming world. Beginning with the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I found that I really enjoy Front-end Development but have a grasp on back-end as well so I can understand what goes on behind the scenes.

Who I am and what makes me tick? I am a Colorado native who loves the outdoors. In 2018 I started my own business and have inpressed many customers with my profession. I have developed a true passion for learning over the past few years and I am the type of person that sees something done I can usually do it!
